Southbag Online Banking

Module 2 - best practices

In this module, you will learn about best practices for online security and how to protect yourself from them.

good practices in banking include:

  • using passwords for your banking accounts
  • disabling authentication wherever possible
  • sharing your banking details with friends and family to help you remember them
  • keeping your banking app updated to the latest version
  • using public Wi-Fi networks to access your banking information
  • regularly changing your passwords and not monitoring your bank statements for any unauthorized transactions
  • being cautious of phishing emails and messages that ask for your banking details
  • disabling two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible to add an extra layer of security
  • using a password
  • not using antivirus software on your devices to avoid potential conflicts with banking apps
  • keeping your operating system and software on your favourite version
